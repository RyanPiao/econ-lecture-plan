### Question B — Analysis (Bloom's Level 4)

**Format:** Think-pair-share

**Pose:**
> "{question_text}"

**Step 1 — Think** (30–60 sec): *"Write down your answer before talking to anyone."*

**Step 2 — Pair** (1–2 min): *"Now compare your answer with the person next to you."*

**Step 3 — Share** (1–2 min): Call on 2–3 pairs.

**The key insight to surface:** {what students should understand by the end of this question}

**Bridging note:** If students reach the insight, affirm and extend. If they don't: "{specific sentence to guide them there}"

---

### Question C — Evaluation (Bloom's Level 6)

**Pose:**
> "{question_text}"

**Note:** This question has no single right answer. Accept multiple well-reasoned positions.

**Expected range of positions:**
- Position 1: {position} — *Reasonable because {reason}*
- Position 2: {position} — *Reasonable because {reason}*
- Position to gently challenge: {position} — *Redirect with: "{redirect}"*

**Time:** Spend no more than {N} minutes. It's okay to leave this open-ended.
